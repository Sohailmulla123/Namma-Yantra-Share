# Namma-Yantra Share 🚜

**Smart Machinery Rental for Smarter Farming**

Namma-Yantra Share is an Android-based agriculture machinery rental application designed to help small-scale farmers access expensive farming equipment such as tractors, harvesters, and power tillers at affordable rental rates.

The platform acts as an **"Uber for Agricultural Machinery"**, allowing machinery owners to list their equipment and enabling farmers to browse, calculate rental prices, and send booking requests directly through the app.

---

## Problem Statement

Small farmers often cannot afford to purchase expensive agricultural machinery.

At the same time, many large farmers own machinery that remains unused for several days every month.

There is currently no simple digital platform that connects machinery owners with farmers who need temporary access.

Namma-Yantra Share solves this problem by creating a peer-to-peer agricultural equipment rental ecosystem.
